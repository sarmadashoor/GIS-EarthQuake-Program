5.0 Taltal-Antofagasta, Chile EARTHQUAKE, March 17, 2013 19:56:30

5.0 Taltal, Antofagasta, Chile Earthqauke. Image and Data Credit USGS.

The USGS has reported a 5.0 magnitude earthquake has struck Taltal, Antofagasta, Chile on . The epicenter was located approximately:

61 km NNW of Taltal, Chile
138 km S of Antofagasta, Chile
176 km NNW of Diego de Almagro, Chile
278 km N of Copiapo, Chile
951 km N of Santiago, Chile

...at a depth of 24.0 km. There were no significant injuries or damage reported at this point, Friendly Forecast will update this report as events unfold.
