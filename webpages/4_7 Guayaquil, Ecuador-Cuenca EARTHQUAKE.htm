4.7 Guayaquil, Ecuador-Cuenca EARTHQUAKE, April 28, 2012 at 07:43:24 PM

4.7 Guayaquil, Ecuador Earthquake. Image and Data Credit USGS.

The USGS has reported a 4.7 magnitude earthquake has struck Guayaquil, Ecuador on Saturday, April 28, 2012 at 07:43:24 PM . The epicenter was located approximately:

42 km (26 miles) ESE of Guayaquil, Ecuador
91 km (56 miles) NW of Cuenca, Ecuador
119 km (73 miles) WSW of Riobamba, Ecuador
253 km (157 miles) SSW of QUITO, Ecuador

...at a depth of 115.3 km (71.6 miles). There were no significant injuries or damage reported at this point, Friendly Forecast will update this report as events unfold. You can read the full Guayaquil, Ecuador earthquake details here.

Did you feel it?

Communities around Guayaquil, Ecuador reported strong shaking. Places that felt the quake are:
City: Samborondón Intensity: III Responses: 3
City: Cuenca Intensity: II Responses: 5
City: Guayaquil Intensity: IV Responses: 9
City: Ambato Intensity: II Responses: 1
If you felt the quake you can report the quake here to the USGS.

What is a 4.7 magnitude earthquake?

4.0 – 4.9 Moderate and noticeable to people indoors, windows rattling, some things may fall off shelves and the sound is similar to a freight train passing by Learn more about earthquakes...
